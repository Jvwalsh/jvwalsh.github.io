About

Welcome to my website. Heres you can learn more about my journey through web development, as well as a thing or two about who I am. Enjoy your time here and reach out with any questions.

I was born and raised in St. Louis. After completing my Chemical Engineering at the Missouri Univeristy of Science and Technology, I quickly gained an interest in web development, particularly web security, blockchain applications, and fullstack projects. During my first year of full-time employemnt, I applied to and attended Fullstack Academy. My on-going journey from chemical engineer to software engineer has been an incredible experience.
